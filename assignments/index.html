# Verbal Quiz Questions

Worth 20% of Final Grade

At the start of nearly every class, your instructor will be calling on you to a ask a few questions about the videos and readings assigned the previous week. You should come prepared with at least two questions that will help clarify the topics that you reviewed. The instructor will ask the first person with their hand raised or select someone at random to ask a question. Then other students will be given the opportunity to try to answer.

The objective is to start a discussion and help each other to better understand the referenced material.

Each helpful question is worth 5 of the 20 marks. Over the course of the semester you need to ask 4 questions to get your full 20%.

Answering one of the questions is worth 2 marks towards the 20%.

# Hybrid Exercises

Worth 15% of Final Grade

The Hybrid exercises are small exercises meant to be completed outside of class hours as part of the asynchronous self-directed portion of the course. You will be provided with references to use to complete each exercise. The Hybrid exercises in this course combine to create one whole project by the end of the semester. There are six parts to the project. Each part builds on the previous parts. You need to complete the first part before you can do the second, and so on.

Don't wait until the day before they are due to start. You will need time too research and understand the material before completing each of the exercises.
